# Drawing number crossreference

4xxx series share a lot of boards between each other, leading to cross-compatibility questions. Additionally, Datron was continuously tweaking their instruments, leading to new board revisions, and sometimes new boards altogether. This document attempts to catalogue various board types, their revisions, and instruments they are (and can be) used in.

## Sources

4708: <https://xdevs.com/doc/Datron/4708/Datron%204708%20Calibration%20and%20servicing%20handbook%20corrected%20V2.pdf>
4808: Datron-Wavetek groups.io Reference Manual
